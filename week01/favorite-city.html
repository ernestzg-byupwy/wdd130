Favorite City

My favorite city is San Miguel, located in the eastern part of El Salvador. The weather is usually hot, although in December and January it might get a little cold and windy early in the mornings.

I like San Salvador too, the capital, because it has more malls and stores to visit, and most of the tech related jobs are in the capital, but I still prefer San Miguel, because the pace is a little slow, less traffic, less yelling, less stress.
